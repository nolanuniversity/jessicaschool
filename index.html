The Name Jessica

Meaning: "To behold," "to see clearly," "foresight" — from the Hebrew

Wealth without purpose is empty. Purpose without wealth is powerless. Here, we teach both.

Who We Are

About the Jessica School

Built to close the gap between wealth creation and wealth law

Most universities teach wealth management and law separately. Students graduate knowing one side of wealth — but not the other.

The Jessica School of Wealth Creation & Law was created to close that gap. We are the first academic institution dedicated entirely to the integrated discipline of wealth creation and wealth law.

Our Mission

To educate and empower a new generation of wealth creators who understand the law, and wealth lawyers who understand creation.
